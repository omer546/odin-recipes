Mac&Cheese

Discription

This recipe will help you make the most delicious tasting MacnCheese you have tried it is easy to follow and does not require that many steps. Best of all it can be completed in a short amount of time so that you and your peers may enjoy it in no time.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) box of elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 Cup Butter
  • 1/2 Cup of All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • 2 Cups of Milk
  • 2 Cups of Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the macaroni
  2. Make the roux using the flour, butter, milk, salt, and pepper
  3. Add cheese to the roux
  4. Drain the boiled macaroni
  5. Add the macaroni to the roux
  6. Mix well and enjoy
