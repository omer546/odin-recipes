Rib-Eye Steak

Discription

This recipe will help you make the most delicious tasting rib-eye Steak you have tried it is easy to follow and does not require that many steps. Best of all it can be completed in a short amount of time so that you and your peers may enjoy the lasagna in no time.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 Cup Maple Syrup
  • 6 Cloves Garlic, Minced
  • 1 Tbs Grated Fresh Ginger
  • 1 Tsp Mustard Powder
  • 1/2 Tsp Sesame Oil
  • 1/4 Tsp Hot Pepper Sauce
  • 4 (10 ounce) beef rib-eye steaks

Steps

  1. Start by combining the soy sauce, maple syrup, garlic, ginger, mustard powder, sesame oil, and hot pepper sauce in a bowl
  2. Prepare steaks by removing any excess fat from them and punching holes with a fork
  3. Place steaks in a casserole dish and pour the previously made marinade over them
  4. Cover them with clear wrap or foil and let them marinate for 1-24 hours
  5. Cover it with a foil and bake
  6. Pre-heat grill, first sear each side for 15 seconds then change sides every 5 minutes based on chosen readiness
