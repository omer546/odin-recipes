Lasagna

Discription

This recipe will help you make the most delicious tasting lasagna you have tried it is easy to follow and does not require that many steps. Best of all it can be completed in a short amount of time so that you and your peers may enjoy the lasagna in no time.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Strips
  • Cheese of your choice
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce and Canned crushed Tomato
  • Vegetables of your liking (Mushrooms, Zucchini, Peppers)

Steps

  1. Start by making the meat Sauce
  2. Now boil the pasta noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover it with a foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna cool off before serving
